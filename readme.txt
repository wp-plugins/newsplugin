== Description ==

[NewsPlugin](http://newsplugin.com/) is the ultimate FREE news plugin for WordPress. Create custom newsfeeds and watch the fresh relevant news headlines appear on your website. Choose keywords, number of articles and other settings, put the feed wherever you want using widgets or shortcodes. You can always shape the news right from your website, remove unwanted articles or star the good ones.

== Installation ==

1. **Install:** Install the NewsPlugin either via the WordPress.org plugin directory, or by uploading the files to your server.
2. **Enter Activation Key:** First of all, enter your Activation Key in the Activate tab.
3. **Generate Shortcode:** Then you can create your newsfeed by generating shortcode from Generate Shortcode tab. Put this shortcode in posts or pages where you want to display your newsfeed.
4. **Create NewsFeed from Widgets:** OR you can create your newsfeed from Appearance > Widgets and from the widgets panel drag the \"NewsPlugin\" widget to the desired sidebar or widget area where you want to show your newsfeed. The NewsPlugin added 4 new widget areas, you can use their shortcodes to show your newsfeed anywhere you want.
5. **Setup/Customize your newsfeed:** Edit the widget features to create/edit your newsfeed. Choose the name, number of headlines, keywords and other settings.
6. **Preview:** Preview your site to see your newsfeed with news headlines.
7. **Edit headlines (if you want to):** You can remove unwanted headlines or star the good ones right from your site. Note that you must be logged in to WordPress as an administrator or an editor to see the Edit Newsfeed Mode link on your page (next to your newsfeed).
8. That's it. You're ready to go!
